Your goal is to replicate the numbers displayed at the top. You can do this by applying operations to the three numbers at the button, such as incrementing or multiplying numbers. Try to do it in as few moves as possible to impress your friends!

keyboard_double_arrow_up
Increment the number on the right.
keyboard_double_arrow_down
Decrement the number on the right.
counter_0
Set number of the right to zero.
swap_horiz
Swap left and right number.
cycle
Rotate the three numbers to the right. The left number will be set to the number that was on the right before.
unknown_2
If number on the right is positive, make it negative, and vice versa.
add
Set the right number to be the left number plus the middle number.
close
Set the right number to be the left number times the middle number.

An operation cannot be performed if one of the numbers would contain three digits after it.
